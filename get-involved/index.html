How to Get Involved

A group including our Connect Core Team , partners and customers, meet monthly to discuss upcoming contributions, areas of improvement, and how to get more people involved.

We meet over Zoom every second Tuesday of the month from 10-11am EST. Anyone who is interested in contributing is welcomed to join.
